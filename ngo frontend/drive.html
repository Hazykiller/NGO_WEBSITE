Our Mission

We launched our Winter Clothing Drive to provide warmth to underprivileged communities. Volunteers collected jackets, blankets, and sweaters from across the city.

Community Participation

More than 300 families contributed clothing items. Local schools, offices, and residential societies came together to make this drive a success.

Impact

We distributed over 1,000 clothing items to 500+ individuals, ensuring they could stay warm through the winter months.
